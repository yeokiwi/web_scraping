### AI Summary
The updated NACLAR Guidelines now explicitly require all institutions with research facilities to establish their own Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC), which is responsible for oversight and evaluation of animal care programs and ensuring compliance with the guidelines. Additionally, the guidelines now mandate that all animal research users undergo appropriate training before conducting experiments, with specific requirements for core curriculum and competencies to be determined by IACUCs in accordance with NACLAR standards.
