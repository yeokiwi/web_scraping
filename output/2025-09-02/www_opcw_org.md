### AI Summary
The recent updates to the Chemical Weapons Convention primarily involve organizational and procedural enhancements rather than substantive changes to the treaty's core obligations. These include the establishment of the Secretariat for the Policy-Making Organs to support decision-making processes and meeting coordination, the creation of the Office of Strategy and Policy to provide cross-cutting policy advice and strategic planning, and clarifications to Article VI regarding verification measures for non-prohibited activities, emphasizing systematic monitoring and on-site inspections for Schedule 1 and 2 chemicals.
