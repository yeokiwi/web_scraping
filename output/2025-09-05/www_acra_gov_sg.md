### AI Summary
Effective for annual periods beginning on 1 January 2023, the Financial Reporting Standards (FRS) have been updated to include all official pronouncements issued up to 31 December 2022, while excluding any new, revised, or amended standards effective after that date. Additionally, under the Accountancy Functions (Consolidation) Act 2022, accounting standards and practice directions previously issued by the Council and not revoked before 1 April 2023 are now continued as if made by the Accounting Standards Committee (ASC) after that date.
