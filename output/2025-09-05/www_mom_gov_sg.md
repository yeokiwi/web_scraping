## Responsible re-employment

### Metadata
- **Published:** 2025-09-05
- **Link:** https://www.mom.gov.sg/employment-practices/re-employment
- **Relevance Score:** 104.5
- **Last Processed:** 2025-09-05T10:28:12.975046
- **Is New:** True

### Keywords Found
- security (content: 4)
- terrorism (content: 3)
- contract (content: 17)
- manpower (content: 12)
- employment (title: 1)
- work passes (content: 8)
- development (content: 5)

### AI Summary
The key changes outlined in this document include the increase of the re-employment age from 67 to 68 effective 1 July 2022, with a further planned increase to 69 from 1 July 2026. Additionally, the minimum retirement age was raised to 63 years from 1 July 2022. The Employment Assistance Payment (EAP) structure was updated, now offering a reduced amount of 2 months' salary (minimum $4,000, maximum $8,500) for employees re-employed for at least 30 months since age 63, while maintaining the standard 3.5 months' payment for others. Revised tripartite guidelines also introduced new provisions for part-time re-employment, higher internal retirement ages, structured career planning, job redesign initiatives, and restructured medical benefits.
