### AI Summary
This document outlines key changes to Singapore's controlled drugs licensing framework, with notable modifications including the introduction of distinct validity periods and application requirements for different product categories: diagnostic test kits now qualify for multi-consignment licenses valid for one year, while therapeutic products, active ingredients, and veterinary products remain restricted to single-consignment, six-month licenses. Additionally, all license applications now require supplementary documentation such as specific dealer's licenses, site layouts, and controlled drug registers, with mandatory pre-license audits and ongoing compliance checks post-approval.
